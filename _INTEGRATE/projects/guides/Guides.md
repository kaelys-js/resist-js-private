## Appendix: What This Library Does NOT Cover

This library is designed for practical, actionable knowledge. It deliberately excludes:

- **Academic theory** — No history, philosophy, or pure science unless directly applicable
- **Professional specialization** — No surgery, advanced engineering, or law practice
- **Weapons manufacturing** — No improvised weapons, explosives, or similar
- **Hacking or exploitation** — No offensive cyber, social engineering playbooks, or surveillance
- **Region-specific law** — Legal sections cover general principles, not jurisdiction-specific statutes
- **Long-term governance** — Community organization is covered; building governments is not

If you need something and it isn't here, it either belongs in a specialized professional reference or it was accidentally missed.
