Christmas Ham

Ingredients (Serves 2)

Ham
	•	9 lb (≈4.1 kg) spiral-cut ham (e.g., Kirkland-style)

Glaze
	•	2 cups brown sugar
	•	4 tablespoons Dijon mustard
	•	6 tablespoons + 2 teaspoons honey
	•	2 tablespoons fresh orange zest
    - 2 teaspoons sherry vinegar

For the Pan
	•	1½ cups water

⸻

Method
	1.	Heat Oven
Preheat to 325°F (160°C). Place ham cut-side down in a large roasting pan. Add water.
	2.	Cover & Warm
Cover tightly with foil. Heat 2 to 2½ hours, until warmed through
(about 15–18 minutes per pound).
	3.	Make Glaze
Gently heat brown sugar, Dijon mustard, honey, and orange zest in a saucepan until smooth and glossy.
	4.	Glaze & Increase Heat
Remove foil. Increase oven to 375°F (190°C). Brush glaze generously, allowing it to drip between slices.
	5.	Caramelize
Return ham to oven 30–40 minutes, uncovered, brushing once or twice more, until well caramelized.
	6.	Finish & Rest
Remove at 140°F (60°C) internal temperature. Rest 15–20 minutes before carving.
