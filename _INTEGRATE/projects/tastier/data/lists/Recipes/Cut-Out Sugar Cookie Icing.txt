Cut-Out Sugar Cookie

Ingredients (Serves 6 | Makes ~24 cookies)

Cookie Dough

12 tablespoons (170 g) unsalted butter, browned and cooled
	•	1 cup (200 g) granulated sugar
	•	1 large whole egg
	•	1 large egg yolk
	•	2⅔ cups (320 g) all-purpose flour
	•	1/2 teaspoon fine sea salt
	•	2 teaspoons vanilla extra

⸻

Method
	1.	Heat Oven
Preheat to 350°F (175°C). Line baking sheets with parchment.
	2.	Brown Butter
Cook butter over medium heat until milk solids turn deep golden and smell nutty.
Transfer to a bowl and cool to room temperature (about 10 minutes).
	3.	Build Base
Whisk browned butter, sugar, and salt until glossy and cohesive.
Mix in egg yolks and vanilla until fully smooth and emulsified.
	4.	Form Dough
Fold in flour just until combined.
Dough should be soft and pliable, not sticky.
Rest 15 minutes to relax gluten.
	5.	Roll & Cut
Roll dough to 1/4 inch (6 mm) thickness on a lightly floured surface.
Cut shapes and transfer to prepared baking sheets.
Chill cut dough 5 minutes if it feels warm.
	6.	Bake
Bake 10–12 minutes, until edges are pale gold and centers are matte and set.
Bake in batches; do not crowd trays.
	7.	Cool
Rest on tray 5 minutes, then transfer to a rack to cool completely before decorating.

⸻

Optional Enhancements
	•	Add citrus zest for subtle brightness
	•	Brush warm cookies lightly with melted butter for sheen
	•	Chill dough overnight for sharper edges
	•	Dust lightly with sugar before baking for sparkle


Cut-Out Sugar Cookie Icing

Ingredients (Makes ~1½ cups icing)
	•	2 cups (300 g) powdered sugar, sifted
	•	2 (60 g) pasteurized egg whites
	•	2 teaspoons fresh lemon juice
	•	¼ teaspoon fine sea salt
	•	1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Egg-free option: use reduced aquafaba (chickpea brine simmered to half volume) in equal weight to egg white.

⸻

Method
	1.	Prep
Ensure bowl and tools are clean, dry, and grease-free.
	2.	Build Base
Beat whites until fluffy
Add egg white to sifted powdered sugar.
Mix slowly with a spatula until a thick, smooth paste forms.
	3.	Balance
Stir in lemon juice and salt until fully dissolved and lightly glossy.
	4.	Finish
Fold in vanilla gently to avoid incorporating air.
Rest 5 minutes to allow bubbles to release.
	5.	Adjust Consistency
	•	Outline icing: thick, holds a soft peak
	•	Flood icing: thin with water ¼ tsp at a time until ribbons settle in ~10 seconds

⸻

Decorating Workflow (24 Cookies)
	•	Pipe outlines first; let set 2–3 minutes
	•	Flood centers and guide icing to edges with a toothpick
	•	Tap cookies gently to release air bubbles
	•	Allow to dry 1–2 hours before stacking

This volume comfortably supports full outlines + controlled flooding on 24 standard 7–8 cm cookies.

⸻

Optional Refinements
	•	Replace 2 tablespoons powdered sugar with toasted powdered sugar for subtle depth
	•	Rest icing 10 minutes before piping for a firmer, cleaner set
	•	In humid conditions, reduce added liquid by ~10%
