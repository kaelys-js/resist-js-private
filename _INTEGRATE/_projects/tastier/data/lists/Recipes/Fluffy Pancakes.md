Fluffy Pancakes

Ingredients (Serves 2 | Makes ~6 pancakes)

Batter
	•	1 cup (120 g) all-purpose flour
	•	3/4 cup (180 ml) milk
	•	1 large egg
	•	2 tablespoons (30 g) butter, browned and cooled slightly
	•	1 tablespoon sugar
	•	1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
	•	1/4 teaspoon salt

⸻

Method
	1.	Brown Butter
Melt butter in a small pan over medium heat until milk solids turn golden and smell nutty. Cool 3 minutes.
	2.	Preheat Pan
Heat a non-stick pan or skillet over medium-low heat.
	3.	Mix Dry Ingredients
Whisk flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt until evenly combined.
	4.	Mix Wet Ingredients
Whisk milk and egg together. Slowly stream in the cooled browned butter while whisking.
	5.	Combine
Gently fold wet ingredients into dry ingredients until just combined.
Batter should be thick with small lumps remaining. Rest 5 minutes.
	6.	Cook Pancakes
Lightly grease pan. Ladle batter to form pancakes.
Cook until bubbles form and edges look set, then flip once and cook until golden.
	7.	Serve Hot
Stack and serve immediately.

⸻

Optional Enhancements
	•	Substitute oat milk and coconut oil for dairy-free pancakes
	•	Add vanilla extract or cinnamon to the batter
	•	Fold in blueberries or chocolate chips after mixing
	•	Finish with maple syrup, honey, or fresh fruit
